Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-11

LibreLog is an offline event logging app. It does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information. All data you enter is stored locally on your device and remains under your control.

Summary

  • No account required — the app works without sign‑in.
  • No data collection — we do not receive your data.
  • No analytics or ads — no tracking SDKs.
  • Local storage — your entries, notes, and timestamps are saved only on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. LibreLog has no servers and does not send your data to us or third parties.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following locally on your device:

  • Event entries (event type, timestamp)
  • Optional notes you add to an entry
  • App settings (e.g., selected event type)

Depending on your Android settings, device‑level backups (e.g., Google backups) may include this local data. We do not have access to those backups.

Permissions

LibreLog does not request sensitive permissions. It operates without access to contacts, location, camera, or microphone.

Third‑Party Services

LibreLog does not embed third‑party analytics, advertising, or social SDKs.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data remains on your device until you delete it. You can remove data by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the app. If device backups include LibreLog data, delete those backups through your device/account provider.

Children’s Privacy

LibreLog is a general‑audience app and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not collect or transmit data, we have no way to identify users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect app changes or legal requirements. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact
